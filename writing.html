keyboard doodles


bring me // return

Bring me to the river, and
drown me in the water, friend.
Let my body drift, then
let your conscious lift, when
you return to your family.

Bring me to the ocean, why
not throw me in the current -- try!
Let my mind die, then
let your soul fly, when
you return to your home.

Bring me to the vulcan, and
end my sullen sulking, friend.
Let my soul ignite, then
feel your life set right, when
you return to your world.
